Odin Recipes


A collection of recipes for any occasion on a budget



Planning for guests is stressful on its own. If you have the desire to provide a great experience for those guests without breaking the bank, I've got a solution.

These recipes can be done with very little prep time and are very cost effective, while bringing dishes that will satisfy any Sunday football get-together.



Homemade Pepperoni Pizza

Pizza, an essential and easy choice for any gathering. If you're up for it, you can make your own pizza for a fraction of the price and fresher than delivery!




Baked BBQ Chicken

When pizza isn't an option for some, these finger-lickin drumsticks are an easy alternative. This recipe is even less hands on, and I was amazed how juicy and flavorful these turn out.




Double Chocolate Brownies

Dessert, the final frontier. Best served with a side of ice cream!
